About

The editor
behind the work

I'm Deepanshi, a content editor with 5+ years of experience working across B2B, SaaS, academic, technical, and publishing content.

I started out writing. That background matters because I don't just fix what's broken. I understand how content is built, which means I know exactly where it can go wrong and how to put it right.

Over the years I've edited everything from books and research articles to SEO blogs, legal documents, and social media copy. If there are words in it, I can make them better.

What I care about most is preserving your voice while improving your content. Good editing should be invisible. You shouldn't be able to tell where the writer ended and the editor began.

Project 01
Scaling Cloud FinOps: Proven Strategies to Accelerate Financial Success
Business / FinTech Book  ·  Developmental Editing, Structural Editing, Proofreading
Book Editing
The Challenge
The manuscript had strong technical foundations but read as a collection of ideas rather than a cohesive book. Chapters overlapped, transitions were absent, and the tone shifted inconsistently, making it difficult for a reader to follow the author's thinking from one section to the next.
What I Changed
  • Rebuilt the chapter sequence to create a logical narrative arc from concept to conclusion.
  • Consolidated overlapping sections and cut repetition that diluted the core arguments.
  • Wrote connecting tissue between chapters so ideas flowed rather than jumped.
  • Standardised tone, voice, and language style across the full manuscript while preserving the author's intent.
  • Proofread for grammar, punctuation, and formatting consistency throughout.
The Result
A structurally sound, publication-ready manuscript that reads as a single cohesive work, with the author's expertise and voice intact throughout. Published by Apress.

Project 02
Medical Book  ·  800+ Pages
Academic Content  ·  Developmental Editing, Proofreading, Style Guide Compliance
Long-Form Editing
The Challenge
A practising doctor had written a comprehensive medical manuscript for submission to a prestigious publishing house. The clinical knowledge was thorough but the manuscript was not yet ready to meet the publisher's editorial and formatting standards.
What I Changed
  • Applied the publisher's style guide consistently across the full 800+ page document.
  • Corrected grammar, punctuation, and sentence construction without altering clinical meaning.
  • Restructured paragraphs for readability, making dense medical content more navigable.
  • Standardised headings, spacing, and layout so the document read as a unified whole.
  • Conducted a full consistency review across chapters before submission.
The Result
A professionally presented, submission-ready manuscript that met the publisher's formatting and editorial requirements, with improved readability that made the clinical content more accessible without compromising accuracy.
